About StudyHub

StudyHub is a comprehensive AI-powered learning platform that combines three powerful tools:

  • PDF Chat: Interactive conversations with your documents
  • Content Summarizer: AI-powered content analysis and summarization
  • Quiz Generator: Automatic quiz creation from your materials
Technologies Used:
  • Flask (Web Framework)
  • LangChain (PDF Processing)
  • FAISS (Vector Search)
  • Google Gemini (AI)
  • Ollama (Local LLM)
  • Bootstrap (UI)
